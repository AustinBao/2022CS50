Instructions

  1. Click "Sign Up" in order to create your account if your new. Else, if you're a returning user, press "Log In"
  2. Once you've finished creating your account, the next step is to Log In
  3. Now that you are logged in, more options should appear in the navigation bar. These include "Add Friend", "Table", and "Summary"
  4. Use "Add Friend" to give you access to your friend's time table
  5. "Table" should be used to create your own time table
  6. Finally, "Summary" will actually display the classes you have with your friend or friends along with the subject and period.
